Frequently Asked Questions

1. What are Envision Steel Doors made of?

Envision Steel Doors are made from high-quality cold-rolled steel, designed to offer exceptional strength, durability, and a smooth finish. We use advanced thermal break technology to provide superior insulation and energy efficiency.

2. How does the thermal efficiency of Envision Steel Doors compare to other doors?

Our steel doors feature a thermal barrier system that significantly reduces heat transfer, ensuring excellent insulation and energy savings. With glazing options like triple-pane glass, we achieve one of the best U-values in the industry.

3. Can I customize the design of my Envision Steel Door?

Yes, Envision Steel Doors offer a variety of customization options, including colors, panel designs, and glazing choices. We can tailor the door to your specific needs and aesthetic preferences.

4. What is the Swiss thermal barrier system used in Envision Steel Doors?

The Montanstahl/Ottostumm Swiss thermal barrier system combines advanced insulation technology with classic steel door designs, optimizing the thermal break to deliver superior energy efficiency without sacrificing durability or aesthetics.

5. Are Envision Steel Doors suitable for both residential and commercial applications?

Yes, Envision Steel Doors are versatile and can be used for both residential and commercial applications. Whether you're looking for energy-efficient doors for your home or a secure entryway for your business, we have solutions to meet your needs.

6. How do I maintain my Envision Steel Door?

To maintain your Envision Steel Door, simply clean it with a soft cloth and a non-abrasive cleaning solution. Avoid harsh chemicals and abrasives that could damage the finish. We also recommend inspecting the door periodically to ensure it's functioning properly.

7. Are Envision Steel Doors secure?

Yes, security is a top priority. Our doors are made with cold-rolled steel, offering excellent strength and durability. In addition, they come with high-quality locking mechanisms to ensure maximum security for your home or business.

8. How long does it take to receive an Envision Steel Door after ordering?

Lead times typically range from 8 to 12 weeks, depending on customization options. For more accurate delivery times, please contact our sales team.

9. Do Envision Steel Doors come with a warranty?

Yes, Envision Steel Doors come with a limited warranty that covers manufacturing defects and material issues.

10. How can I get a quote or order an Envision Steel Door?

You can request a quote or place an order by contacting our sales team via phone, email, or by filling out the online form on our website. We're here to assist with pricing, customization, and any questions you may have.
